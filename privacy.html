Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

1. Information Collection

CarModo is designed with privacy-first principles. We do not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number. The App does not require user accounts or manual registration.

When using CarModo, users may upload photos of their vehicles to generate AI-powered car modification visuals. The uploaded content is used solely to perform the requested generation task.

2. Tracking and Analytics

We use Meta (Facebook) SDK to measure app performance, understand user interactions, and improve advertising effectiveness.

With your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, we may collect and process:

  • App interactions (e.g., app opens, purchases, feature usage)
  • Device identifiers such as the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA)
  • Advertising performance data

This data may be used to:

  • Measure advertising campaign performance
  • Optimize user acquisition strategies
  • Deliver more relevant ads

Tracking is only enabled if you explicitly grant permission via the ATT prompt. If you choose not to allow tracking, no tracking data will be collected.

3. Image Data Usage and Processing

CarModo processes vehicle images provided by users to generate AI-based modification previews. Processing is strictly limited to visual transformation requested by the user.

Image generation is performed using AI models including Google Nano Banana, Nano Banana Pro, and Veo 3.1, orchestrated via fal.ai and infrastructure services provided by Supabase.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Uploaded photos and generated content are stored to allow users to access their generated results. Generated images are retained for a maximum period of one (1) year.

Data is temporarily stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted on AWS (Asia Pacific – Sydney, ap-southeast-2).

Users may delete generated images at any time via the Settings section of the App. Once deleted, the content is permanently removed.

5. Third-Party Services

CarModo uses the following third-party services:

  • Meta (Facebook) SDK – analytics and advertising measurement
  • Supabase – backend infrastructure
  • fal.ai – AI processing
  • Google AI models – content generation
  • RevenueCat – subscription management

These services may process limited technical data necessary to provide their functionality.

6. Payments and Subscription Data

Payments are handled via Apple In-App Purchases and managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment information.

7. Data Security

We apply industry-standard measures to protect your data.

8. User Rights

You can:

  • Delete your generated content anytime
  • Disable tracking via iOS Settings

9. Children’s Privacy

CarModo is not intended for children under 9.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed.
