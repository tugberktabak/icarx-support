Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to iCarX ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device Identifier: A unique anonymous identifier to manage your account and credits.
  • Uploaded Images: Photos you upload of vehicles for AI modification processing.
  • Generated Images: AI-modified images created based on your requests.
  • Purchase Information: Transaction data related to in-app purchases, processed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI vehicle modification service
  • Process your uploaded images through our AI models
  • Manage your credit balance and purchase history
  • Improve and optimize our application
  • Communicate with you about your account or our services
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

Image Processing

When you upload a vehicle image:

  • Images are transmitted securely to our servers
  • Images are processed by AI models to generate modifications
  • Original and generated images may be temporarily stored for processing
  • We do not use your images for training AI models without explicit consent
  • Images are not shared with third parties except as necessary for processing

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing
  • AI Processing Services: For generating vehicle modifications

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Generated images: Stored in your history until you delete them
  • Transaction records: Retained for legal and accounting purposes
  • Uploaded images: Temporarily stored during processing, then deleted

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at icarx.app@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
