Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

1. Information Collection

CarModo is designed with privacy-first principles. We do not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location. The App does not require user accounts or manual registration.

When using CarModo, users may upload photos of their vehicles to generate AI-powered car modification visuals. The uploaded content is used solely to perform the requested generation task and is not used for identification, profiling, or tracking purposes.

2. Image Data Usage and Processing

CarModo processes vehicle images provided by users to generate AI-based modification previews. Processing is strictly limited to visual transformation and generation requested by the user.

Image generation is performed using AI models including Google Nano Banana, Nano Banana Pro, and Veo 3.1, orchestrated via fal.ai and infrastructure services provided by Supabase.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Uploaded photos and generated content are stored to allow users to access their generated results. Generated images are retained for a maximum period of one (1) year.

Data is temporarily stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) Asia Pacific – Sydney (ap-southeast-2).

Users may permanently delete generated images at any time via the Settings section of the App. Once deleted, the content is irreversibly removed from our systems.

If the user does not manually delete the generated content, it is automatically deleted after one (1) year. This deletion includes removal from all processing and storage systems, including fal.ai.

No image data is retained beyond this one-year retention period.

4. Third-Party Services

CarModo uses the following third-party services strictly to operate core functionality:

  • Supabase – backend infrastructure and temporary data handling (AWS Asia Pacific – Sydney, ap-southeast-2)
  • fal.ai – AI image generation orchestration
  • Google AI models (Nano Banana, Nano Banana Pro, Veo 3.1) – AI content generation
  • RevenueCat – subscription and purchase management

User images and generated content are not shared with third parties for advertising, marketing, analytics, or profiling. Data is transmitted only to the above services for the sole purpose of providing the requested AI generation.

5. Payments and Subscription Data

All payments and subscriptions are handled securely through Apple In-App Purchases and managed via RevenueCat. CarModo does not collect or store payment details, billing information, or financial identifiers.

6. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect data during processing. Because uploaded vehicle images are deleted after a defined retention period, long-term exposure risk is minimized.

7. User Rights

Users have full control over their generated content and may delete it at any time. Since all generated content is automatically deleted after one year, no additional deletion requests are required.

8. Children’s Privacy

CarModo is not intended for children under the age of 9. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
