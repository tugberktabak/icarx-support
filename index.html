‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is iCarX?

iCarX is an AI-powered app that lets you visualize vehicle modifications before making them in real life. Upload a photo of your car and see how different modifications would look.

How do credits work?

Each AI-generated modification costs 1 credit. New users receive 1 free credit to try the app. Additional credits can be purchased in-app, and subscription plans provide credits automatically each period.

Do credits expire?

No! One-time purchased credits never expire. Subscription credits are added each billing period.

Can I get a refund?

If there's an issue with your generated image, credits may be automatically refunded. For purchase refunds, please contact Apple Support through your purchase history.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the paywall screen and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, or through the App Store.

üîß Troubleshooting

Images not generating? Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, your credit will be automatically refunded.
